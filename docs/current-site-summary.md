# Current Site Summary — Schreinerei Markus Rosch

Schreinerei Markus Rosch is a family-run carpentry business in Duisburg-Neudorf with the trust signal “Familienbetrieb seit 1960”. The company positions itself as an experienced local partner for carpentry work in houses and properties, especially individual furniture construction, plastic and wooden windows, and interior work.

The business presents itself as personal, experienced, local, and approachable. The tone is straightforward, traditional, and trustworthy rather than modern marketing-heavy.

## Core services
- Möbelbau
- Terrassen & Balkon
- Verkleidungen
- Türen & Fenster
- Einbruchsicherheit
- Böden & Decken
- (Klein-)Reparaturen
- Rolladenbau
- Beratung
